      How It Works

      Three-stage AI pipeline for accurate defect detection

      01

      Image Upload

      Upload any solar panel photo. The system accepts JPG, JPEG, and PNG formats.

      02

      YOLOv8 Detection

      CSPDarknet CNN backbone extracts features. The detection head draws precise bounding boxes.

      03

      Damage Analysis

      Confidence scores are mapped to damage percentages. Severity and repair steps are generated.

      Detectable Defect Classes

      The model is trained to identify 6 distinct panel conditions

      Clean

      No defects. Panel operating at optimal efficiency.

      0% Damage

      Dusty

      Dust accumulation reducing light transmission by 5–25%.

      Up to 35%

      Bird Drop

      Bird droppings causing hot-spots and coating corrosion.

      Up to 60%

      Snow Covered

      Snow/ice blocking sunlight. Near-zero output until cleared.

      Up to 50%

      Physical Damage

      Cracks, chips, or impact marks compromising cell integrity.

      Up to 90%

      Electrical Damage

      Burn marks or arc damage. Immediate shutdown required.

      Up to 95%
